Kevin Levin

Kevin levin starts as an antagonist in the series but later becomes one of Ben's strongest allies. His character development is one of the best parts of the show.

Kevin has the ability to absorb materials and energy, allowing him to transform his body into different substances like metal or stone.

Over time, Kevin learns to control his powers and works alongside Ben and Gwen to fight enemies. His rough personality slowly softens as he becomes more loyal and protective.

Kevin represents redemption and growth, making him a fan-favorite character.
