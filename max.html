Grandpa Max

Grandpa Max is the grandfather of Ben and Gwen and a key mentor figure in the series. He is a former member of the plumbers, an intergalactic law enforcement organization.

Max has a vast amount of knowledge about aliens, technology, and the universe. He guides Ben and Gwen throughout their journey, helping them understand threats and how to deal with them.

Despite his age, Max is brave, skilled, and always ready to fight when needed. His experience makes him one of the most important characters in the story.

He also adds a sense of wisdom and stability to the team, balancing the younger characters.
