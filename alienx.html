Alien X

Alien X is considered the most powerful alien in the Ben 10 universe. he belongs to the celestialsapien species and has the ability to control reality itself.

He can manipulate time, space , matter and even rewrite the existence.However, his power comes with a limitation. Inside Alien X exists multiple personalities that must agree before any action is taken.

He represents the concept of ultimate power balanced by decision and control.
