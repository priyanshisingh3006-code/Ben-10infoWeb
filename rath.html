Rath

Rath is one of the most entertaining aliens in Ben 10. He is known for his extreme strength, aggressive behaviour and loud personality.

Rath constantly shouts and argues, often saying "let me tell you something," before going into a rant. His attitude makes him both funny and intimidating.

In battle, Rath relies on brutle strength,speed and durability. He prefers direct combat and has no fear when facing enemies.

Despite his anger issues, Rath is loyal and always fights for the right side, making him one of the most loved aliens.
