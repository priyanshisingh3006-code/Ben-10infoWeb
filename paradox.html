Professor paradox

professor paradox is one of the most unique characters in the Ben 10 universe. He exists outside of normal time and space, making him effectively timeless.

Originally a scientist, an experiment caused him to become unstuck from time. This allowed him to travel across different timelines, and realities.

paradox plays a major role in explaining complax concepts like time travel, mulltivers, and time loops. He often appears at critical moments to guide the horoes.

His calm personality and deep understanding of the universe make him a mysterious but essential character.
